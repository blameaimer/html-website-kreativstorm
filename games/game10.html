Regarded as a pioneer in the first-person shooter genre, "Half-Life 2" remains an influential classic. Players return as Gordon Freeman, thrust into a dystopian world controlled by the alien Combine. The game's exceptional storytelling, innovative physics-based gameplay, and immersive atmosphere continue to leave an indelible mark on the gaming industry.
